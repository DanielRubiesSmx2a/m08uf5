Death Note

Death Note (stylized as DEATH NOTE) is a Japanese manga series written
by Tsugumi Ohba and illustrated by Takeshi Obata.

Plot

In Tokyo, a disaffected high-school student named Light Yagami finds the "Death Note", a mysterious black
notebook that can kill anyone as long as the user knows both the target's name and face. Initially terrified
of its god-like power, Light considers the possibilities of the Death Note's abilities and kills high-profile
Japanese criminals, then targets international criminals.
